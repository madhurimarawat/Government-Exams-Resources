## 🏦 SEBI Grade A <a id="sebi"></a>

🔗 https://www.sebi.gov.in/

- Notification & Applications Start, 30 Oct 2025  
- Last Date, 28 Nov 2025  

### 📅 Timeline
- Admit Card, 04 Jan 2026  
- Prelims Exam, 10 Jan 2026  
- Result, 30 Jan 2026  
- Scorecard, 04 Feb to 21 Feb 2026  
- Mains Admit Card, 12 Feb to 21 Feb 2026  
- Mains Exam, 21 Feb 2026  

🔗 Result, https://www.sebi.gov.in/sebiweb/about/AboutAction.do?doResults=yes  

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Prelims to mains gap is short, prepare mains in advance.
