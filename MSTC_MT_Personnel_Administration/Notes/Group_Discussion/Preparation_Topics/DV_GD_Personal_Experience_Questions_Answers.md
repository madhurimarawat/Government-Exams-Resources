# 📝 MSTC GD Experience

### 🔹 Document Verification (DV)

* Reached DV by **10:00 AM**.

* DV was scheduled around **11:30 AM**.

* Process started later with:

  * Call letter check
  * Identity card verification

* Entry was given after verification.

* Candidates signed the register.

* Candidates who had filled the form were allowed for **biometric verification**.

* A **biometric seal** was put on the call letter.

* After that, **document verification** was done.

* It is advisable to:

  * **Self-attest all photocopies with date beforehand**
  * This makes the process very quick

* After verification:

  * Officials **put a seal and signature** on documents

---

### 🔹 GD Process Entry

* GD timing was also around **11:30 AM**.

* At the gate, they checked:

  * Call letter with seal
  * Aadhaar card from previous DV

* Candidates were seated in a hall.

* They called candidates **group-wise**.

---

### 🔹 GD Setup

* Total candidates: **10**
* Seating: **Circular arrangement on chairs**
* Moderators were sitting in front

---

# 🟡 Key Learnings

---

### 🔹 1. Importance of Real-Life Examples

* I felt that:

  * Real-life examples and data are **more impactful** than theories

* Not that:

  * Bookish knowledge is bad

* But in a discussion:

  * Practical examples help people:

    * Connect better
    * Understand better

---

### 🔹 2. Body Language Matters a Lot

* I realized:

  * **Body language is very important**, sometimes more than what you say

* Observation:

  * Some candidates had very good points
  * But their body language:

    * Was closed
    * Looked like they were speaking to themselves

* Another observation:

  * Many people were looking only at the **moderator**

* This should be avoided:

  * You should look at **everyone in the group**

---

### 🔹 3. My Interaction with the Group

* I tried to:

  * Interact actively with others

* While speaking, I:

  * Asked for opinions
  * Built on others’ points
  * Looked at everyone

* While listening, I:

  * Paid attention
  * Nodded my head
  * Smiled when appropriate

* I also:

  * Let others finish speaking

---

### 🔹 4. Personal Strength (Planned & Executed)

* This approach was:

  * Something I had **planned before the GD**

* I am happy that:

  * It **actually worked in the discussion**

* This is something I am:

  * Proud of

---

### 🔹 5. Advice for Future GDs

* I would recommend:

  * Use **real-life examples**
  * Maintain **open body language**
  * Interact with the group

---

### 🔹 6. Communication Style

* While speaking:

  * Avoid using **very heavy or complex words**

* Instead:

  * Use vocabulary that is:

    * Impressive
    * Yet simple and understandable

* Reason:

  * GD is a **discussion**, not a speech
