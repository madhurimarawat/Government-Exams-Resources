## 🧠 MSTC Management Trainee – Exam Structure & Pattern

### 📝 Selection Process

1. **Computer Based Test (CBT)** – Online Written Test
2. **Group Discussion (GD)** – Only for candidates who pass CBT
3. **Personal Interview** – For final selection
   ➡️ Final merit is based on performance in CBT, GD and Interview.

---

## 📊 CBT (Written Test) Pattern

| Part      | Section                    | Questions | Marks   | Duration    |
| --------- | -------------------------- | --------- | ------- | ----------- |
| A         | English Language           | 15        | 15      | 60 min      |
| A         | Quantitative Aptitude      | 20        | 20      |             |
| A         | Reasoning Ability          | 20        | 20      |             |
| A         | General Knowledge          | 15        | 15      |             |
| B         | **Professional Knowledge** | 50        | 50      | 60 min      |
| **Total** |                            | **120**   | **120** | **120 min** |

✅ **Negative Marking:** 0.25 marks deducted per wrong answer.

## 📘 Detailed Syllabus (Topics to Cover)

### 1️⃣ English Language

* Reading Comprehension
* Cloze Test
* Error Spotting
* Sentence Correction
* Para Jumbles
* Vocabulary (Synonyms/Antonyms)
* Usage of Articles/Prepositions

### 2️⃣ Quantitative Aptitude

* Number System
* Percentages
* Ratio & Proportion
* Simplification & Approximation
* Time, Speed & Distance
* Profit & Loss
* Time & Work
* Averages
* Data Interpretation basics
* Mensuration
* Probability

### 3️⃣ Reasoning Ability

* Coding – Decoding
* Blood Relations
* Seating Arrangement (basic)
* Series (Number/Alphabet)
* Logical Deduction
* Syllogism
* Input–Output
* Directions

### 4️⃣ General Knowledge / Awareness

* Current Affairs (National & International)
* Indian Economy basics
* Government Schemes
* Science & Technology
* PSU Sector Knowledge
* Important Awards, Sports

### 5️⃣ Professional Knowledge (Most Important)

**Depends on Discipline/Cadre:**

#### 🧩 For **Operations / General Cadre**

* Planning & Scheduling
* Project Management Fundamentals
* Statistical Analysis & Decision Making
* Managerial Economics
* Marketing Management basics
* Organizational Behaviour
* Business Communication concepts

#### 💼 For **Finance & Accounts Cadre**

* Financial Accounting Principles
* Cost & Management Accounting
* Corporate Finance basics
* Budgeting & Financial Management
* Auditing
* Taxation fundamentals
* Business Laws (Company Act basics)
* Capital Market concepts

> Note: Official MSTC syllabus defines Part B as based on **functional/ professional knowledge** relevant to the post.

## 📌 Preparation Notes & Study Tips

### 📍 For Aptitude & Reasoning

* Practice **daily 30–40 questions**
* Focus on data interpretation and simplification
* Use previous PSU exam papers for practice

### 📍 English

* Read editorials and practice comprehension daily
* Learn common grammar rules and vocabulary lists

### 📍 General Knowledge

* Daily current affairs (last 6–12 months)
* Important national & international events
* PSU sector terminology

### 📍 Professional Knowledge (Key Part)

* Go through basic textbooks or online notes related to the cadre:

  * For **Finance** – Accounting, Auditing, Taxation
  * For **Operations/Management** – Project management basics, business studies topics
* Practice MCQs from PSU-level finance and management topics

## 📚 Study Materials & Notes (Recommendations)

### 📘 Books / Resources

* **Quantitative Aptitude** – R.S. Aggarwal / Arun Sharma
* **English Language** – Wren & Martin Grammar, English practice papers
* **Reasoning** – R.S. Aggarwal’s reasoning
* **General Knowledge/Current Affairs** – Monthly magazines (Pratiyogita Darpan / GK updates)
* **Professional Knowledge**

  * **Finance** – “Corporate Finance”, “Financial Accounting” notes
  * **Business/Operations** – MBA level textbooks on Marketing, Organizational Behaviour, Project Management

### 📑 Practice Tests

* Attempt **mock tests** for CBT pattern
* Solve previous year questions from govt/PSU exams
* Focus on **timed practice** (120 questions in 120 min)
