## 2️⃣ Key Agricultural Facts

| 🌱 Topic              | Quick Facts                                                           |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Crops by Season      | Kharif: Rice, Maize, Cotton, Sugarcane <br> Rabi: Wheat, Barley, Gram |
| Major Livestock      | Cattle, Buffalo, Sheep, Goat, Poultry                                 |
| Horticulture         | Fruits: Mango, Banana, Citrus <br> Vegetables: Potato, Tomato, Onion  |
| Agroforestry         | Trees + crops integration; soil & income benefit                      |
| Fertilizers & Inputs | Urea, DAP, NPK, micro-nutrients                                       |
| Organic Farming      | Promoted under Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana                       |
| Agri-Insurance       | PMFBY; covers yield loss due to natural calamities                    |

## 3️⃣ Rural Banking & NABARD Focus

| 🏦 Topic                  | Quick Facts                                                                         |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| NABARD Functions         | Refinance for rural credit, SHG-Bank linkage, infrastructure funding, agri-research |
| SHG-Bank Linkage         | Women SHGs; promotes savings + credit; social & financial inclusion                 |
| Co-op Banks & RRBs       | NABARD regulates, supervises, refinances                                            |
| Priority Sector Lending  | Agriculture, MSME, Renewable Energy, Export Credit, SHGs                            |
| Microfinance & SHG Loans | NABARD provides refinance to MFIs for lending to rural poor                         |
| Credit Planning          | District Credit Plans; Annual Credit Plans (ACP)                                    |

## 4️⃣ Rural Infrastructure & Development Programs

| 🏗️ Program              | Key Points                                                                 |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Rural Roads (PMGSY)    | Connectivity; road quality & maintenance; funds by central/state govt      |
| Rural Drinking Water   | Jal Jeevan Mission; piped water to households                              |

| Skill Development      | DDU-GKY; rural youth employment & entrepreneurship                         |

| Agricultural Marketing | e-NAM, FPOs, cold storage, rural mandis                                    |
| Digital Inclusion      | CSCs (Common Service Centers), e-governance, DBT (Direct Benefit Transfer) |

## 5️⃣ NABARD & Rural Development Quick Memory Hacks

* 🌾 **Schemes** – Use **acronym + year**

  * Example: **PM-KISAN 2019**, **PMFBY 2016**, **NRLM 2011**, **PMAY-G 2016**
* 🏦 **Banking linkage** – **SHG, Co-op, RRB, Priority Sector**
* 💡 **Focus areas** – Rural credit, livelihoods, agriculture insurance, irrigation, skill development
* 📊 **Agriculture cycles** – Kharif: rice & cotton; Rabi: wheat & pulses
* 🏗️ **Infrastructure** – Roads, water, electrification, digital services
* 🎯 **Insurance schemes** – PMFBY (crops), livestock insurance under NABARD
* 🔄 **Integrated memory trick**: "**Rural Roads, Health, Skill, SHG-Bank, Crop Insurance**" → **RHS-SC**
