# 📘 SEBI Officer Grade **A** (General Stream): Syllabus & Scheme

*Recruitment Notification: Officer Grade A - General Stream (2025)*
**Exam Phases:** Phase I → Phase II → Interview (Phase III) 

---

## 🗂️ 1. 📅 Exam Scheme Overview

| **Stage**     | **Type**  | **Papers**                                           | **Marks** | **Duration** |
| ------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | --------- | ------------ |
| **Phase I**   | Screening | Paper I (Common)                                     | 100       | 60 min       |
|               |           | Paper II (General Stream)                            | 100       | 40 min       |
| **Phase II**  | Main Exam | Paper I (Descriptive – English)                      | 100       | 60 min       |
|               |           | Paper II (Multiple Choice – General Stream Subjects) | 100       | 90 min       |
| **Phase III** | Interview | Personal Interview                                   | –         | –            |

**Negative Marking:** 0.25 marks for each wrong MCQ in Phase I & Phase II. 

---

## 🧠 2. 📌 Phase I: **Screening Examination**

### 🧾 Paper I: **Common for All Streams**

**Total:** 💯 Marks | **Duration:** 🕐 60 minutes
**Subjects Covered:**

* 📊 **General Awareness** (incl. Financial Sector basics)
* 🗣️ **English Language**
* 📈 **Quantitative Aptitude**
* 🧩 **Reasoning Ability** 

> *This paper evaluates fundamental reasoning, quantitative skills, language ability and awareness of current events & markets.*

---

### 📘 Paper II: **General Stream - MCQ**

**Total:** 💯 Marks | **Duration:** 🕐 40 minutes
**Topics (General Stream):**

* 🧮 Commerce & Accountancy
* 📋 Management Principles
* 💰 Finance & Costing
* 📑 Companies Act
* 📊 Economics

> *This tests core knowledge related to business, financial markets, and regulatory framework.* 

---

## 🖋️ 3. 📘 Phase II: **Main Examination**

### ✍️ Paper I: **English (Descriptive)**

**Total:** 💯 Marks | **Duration:** 🕐 60 minutes
**Focus Areas:**

* 📝 Essay writing on contemporary topics
* 📄 Precis writing
* 📘 Reading comprehension & analysis

Purpose: *Evaluate drafting ability, clarity of thought and communication skills.* 

---

### 📚 Paper II: **General Stream - MCQ**

**Total:** 💯 Marks | **Duration:** 🕐 90 minutes
**Advanced Syllabus Topics:**
✔️ **Commerce & Accountancy**
✔️ **Management Concepts & Organizational Behaviour**
✔️ **Finance & Costing Techniques**
✔️ **Companies Act Provisions**
✔️ **Economic Theory & Market Structure**

> *Higher complexity and deeper testing compared to Phase I.* 

---

## 👔 4. 🧑‍💼 Phase III: **Interview (Viva Voce)**

✔ Only candidates shortlisted from Phase II will be called.
✔ Focus on analytical skills, domain knowledge, attitude & decision-making. 

---

## 🎯 5. 📖 Recommended Syllabus Breakdown (Generalist)

### 📌 General Awareness

* National & International Current Affairs
* Financial Markets & Institutions
* Banking – RBI Functions
* Indian Economy & Budget Highlights
* Important Government Policies

---

### 📌 English Language

* Grammar & Vocabulary
* Reading Comprehension
* Para-jumbles
* Error Spotting & Sentence Improvement

---

### 📌 Quantitative Aptitude

* Number Systems & Series
* Ratio & Proportion
* Time, Speed & Distance
* Percentages, Averages
* Data Interpretation & Basic Algebra

---

### 📌 Reasoning Ability

* Logical Reasoning & Puzzles
* Seating Arrangements
* Coding-Decoding
* Blood Relations
* Syllogism & Input-Output

---

### 📌 Commerce & Accountancy

* Financial Accounting Principles
* Bookkeeping & Final Accounts
* Balance Sheet Analysis
* Cost Accounting Fundamentals

---

### 📌 Management & Finance

* Organizational Behaviour
* Marketing Principles
* Working Capital Management
* Financial Ratios & Funding Instruments

---

### 📌 Companies Act & Economics

* Company Formation & Structure
* Directors & Shareholders Duties
* Micro & Macro Economics
* Demand–Supply & Market Structures

---

## 🧠 Preparation Tips

**A SEBI candidate should:**

🔹 Build a daily routine covering Current Affairs, English and basics of markets.

🔹 Focus on conceptual clarity before solving mocks.

🔹 Revise key economics and finance frameworks weekly.

🔹 Practice past papers & descriptive writing for Phase II.
