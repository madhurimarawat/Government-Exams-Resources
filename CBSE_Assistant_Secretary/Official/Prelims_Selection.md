# 🧾 CBSE DRQ 2026 – **Prelims (Tier-1) Cut-off & Merit Explained Simply**

## 🔹 1️⃣ What happens in **Prelims (Tier-1)?**

* Tier-1 is a **screening exam**
* It is used to:
  👉 **shortlist candidates for Tier-2**
* **Final selection is NOT based on Tier-1 marks alone**

Think of Tier-1 as a **filter** 🚿

---

## 🔹 2️⃣ Is there a fixed cut-off mark?

❌ **No fixed number is announced in advance**

✔️ The cut-off is decided **after the exam**, based on:

* Difficulty of paper
* Number of vacancies
* Performance of candidates

---

## 🔹 3️⃣ Minimum qualifying rule (VERY IMPORTANT ⚠️)

👉 **Everyone must score at least 30% marks**

If total marks = **100**

| Category       | Minimum marks needed |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| All categories | **30 marks**         |

❌ If you score **below 30**, you are **OUT**, even if others scored low.

---

## 🔹 4️⃣ How is the actual cut-off decided? (Category-wise)

CBSE first decides the **UR (General) cut-off** 🧠
Then other categories are calculated from that.

### 📌 Rule:

* **UR cut-off = Base**
* Others get **percentage relaxation**

---

## 🔹 5️⃣ Category relaxation explained (with emojis)

| Category         | Cut-off rule         |
| ---------------- | -------------------- |
| 🟦 UR (General)   | Full cut-off         |
| 🟩 OBC / EWS      | **5% less than UR**  |
| 🟪 SC / ST / PwBD | **10% less than UR** |

---

## 🔹 6️⃣ Example calculation 🧮 (VERY IMPORTANT)

### Suppose:

* Tier-1 total marks = **100**
* UR cut-off decided = **60 marks**

### Then 👇

| Category         | Calculation    | Final cut-off |
| ---------------- | -------------- | ------------- |
| 🟦 UR             | 60             | **60**        |
| 🟩 OBC / EWS      | 60 − 5% of 60  | **57**        |
| 🟪 SC / ST / PwBD | 60 − 10% of 60 | **54**        |

⚠️ Remember:

* Even if relaxation applies, **30% minimum rule still applies**

---

## 🔹 7️⃣ How many people are selected from Tier-1?

CBSE calls candidates in a **ratio**, usually:

### 👉 **1 : 10**

Means:

* If **100 vacancies**
* About **1000 candidates** are shortlisted for Tier-2

📌 Selection is **post-wise**, not overall.

---

## 🔹 8️⃣ Is Tier-1 merit list final?

❌ **NO**

✔️ Tier-1 is only for:

* **Shortlisting**

✔️ Final merit depends on:

* Tier-2
* Interview / Skill test (if applicable)

---

## 🔹 9️⃣ Can CBSE change cut-off later?

✔️ **YES**

CBSE has the right to:

* Lower cut-off
* Adjust category numbers
  👉 if enough candidates are not available

---

## 🧠 One-line Memory Trick

> **Tier-1 = Filter, not Final**
> **UR sets the bar, others get relaxation**
> **30% is compulsory for everyone**
