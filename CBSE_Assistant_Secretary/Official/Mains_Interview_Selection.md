# 🧾 CBSE DRQ 2026 – **Mains (Tier-2) & Interview**

## 🧠 PART 1: **MAINS (Tier-2)**

---

## 🔹 1️⃣ What is **Mains (Tier-2)?**

* Tier-2 is the **MAIN deciding exam** 🏆
* It tests:

  * Subject knowledge
  * Professional ability
* **Tier-2 marks ARE counted for final merit**

👉 Unlike Prelims, **Tier-2 really matters**

Think of Tier-2 as the **real competition stage** 🎯

---

## 🔹 2️⃣ Who is allowed to appear for Tier-2?

✔️ Only those who:

* Clear **Tier-1 cut-off**
* Score **at least 30%** in Tier-1

📌 Tier-1 → Entry gate
📌 Tier-2 → Ranking stage

---

## 🔹 3️⃣ Minimum qualifying rule in Tier-2 (VERY IMPORTANT ⚠️)

👉 **Everyone must score at least 30% marks in Tier-2**

Example:

If Tier-2 total marks = **300**

| Category       | Minimum marks needed |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| All categories | **90 marks**         |

❌ If you score **below 30%**, you are **DISQUALIFIED**
(even if vacancies are there)

---

## 🔹 4️⃣ Tier-2 Cut-off (Category-wise)

Same logic as Prelims 👇

### 📌 Rule:

* **UR cut-off = Base**
* Others get relaxation

| Category         | Cut-off rule         |
| ---------------- | -------------------- |
| 🟦 UR (General)   | Full cut-off         |
| 🟩 OBC / EWS      | **5% less than UR**  |
| 🟪 SC / ST / PwBD | **10% less than UR** |

---

## 🔹 5️⃣ Tier-2 Cut-off Example 🧮

### Suppose:

* Tier-2 total marks = **300**
* UR cut-off = **180**

### Then 👇

| Category         | Calculation    | Final cut-off |
| ---------------- | -------------- | ------------- |
| 🟦 UR             | 180            | **180**       |
| 🟩 OBC / EWS      | 180 − 5% (9)   | **171**       |
| 🟪 SC / ST / PwBD | 180 − 10% (18) | **162**       |

⚠️ Still:

* **30% minimum (90 marks)** applies to all

---

## 🔹 6️⃣ How many are shortlisted after Tier-2?

Depends on the **post** 👇

* Some posts → **Interview**
* Some posts → **Skill Test**
* Some posts → **Tier-2 only (final merit)**

📌 Tier-2 shortlisting is **stricter than Tier-1**

---

## 🧠 PART 2: **INTERVIEW / SKILL TEST**

## 🔹 7️⃣ Is interview for all posts?

❌ **NO**

✔️ Interview is conducted **only for certain posts**
(e.g. Assistant Secretary, Accounts Officer, etc.)

✔️ Other posts may have:

* Skill Test
* Typing Test
* No interview at all

📌 Check post-specific scheme

---

## 🔹 8️⃣ Does interview have cut-off?

❌ **No fixed cut-off**

✔️ BUT:

* Interview marks are **added to Tier-2**
* Very low interview marks can **push you down in merit**

Think of interview as:
🧂 Salt in food — small, but important

---

## 🔹 9️⃣ How final merit is prepared 🏁

### 📌 Final Merit depends on the post:

### ✅ Case 1: Posts **with Interview**

Final merit =
👉 **Tier-2 marks + Interview marks**

### ✅ Case 2: Posts **with Skill Test**

* Skill Test = **Qualifying only**
* Final merit = **Tier-2 marks**

### ✅ Case 3: Posts **without Interview/Skill**

* Final merit = **Tier-2 marks only**

---

## 🔹 🔟 Can CBSE change rules later?

✔️ **YES**

CBSE can:

* Lower cut-off
* Change shortlisting ratio
* Adjust category numbers

👉 If enough candidates are not available

---

# 🧠 FINAL MEMORY SUMMARY (EXAM GOLD 🏆)

> **Tier-1 = Screening (Filter)**
>
> **Tier-2 = Ranking (Main game)**
>
> **Interview = Add-on, not eliminator**
>
> **30% rule applies EVERYWHERE**
>
> **Final merit mostly depends on Tier-2**
