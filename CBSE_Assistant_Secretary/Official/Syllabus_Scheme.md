# 🏛️ CBSE Recruitment – Assistant Secretary  
## 📚 Syllabus & Examination Scheme (Outline)

---

## 1. About CBSE Assistant Secretary

The **Assistant Secretary** is a **Group-A administrative post** under the **Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)**.  

The role involves:
- 📝 **Policy implementation & coordination**
- 🏫 **Examination management**
- 📊 **Governance support**

Assistant Secretaries work closely with senior officials in planning, execution, and monitoring of CBSE’s academic, examination, and regulatory functions.

- Education sector developments

- Government organizational structure
