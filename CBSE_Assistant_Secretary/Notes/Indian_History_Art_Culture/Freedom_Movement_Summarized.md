# 🇮🇳 PART 1: INDIAN FREEDOM MOVEMENT (1857–1947)

---

## 🔹 1️⃣ Revolt of 1857 (First War of Independence)

### 📌 Why it happened?

* British exploitation
* Doctrine of Lapse
* Greased cartridges issue

### 🔥 Important leaders:

* **Mangal Pandey** – Barrackpore
* **Rani Lakshmibai** – Jhansi
* **Bahadur Shah Zafar** – Delhi
* **Kunwar Singh** – Bihar

🧠 *Result:*
❌ Revolt failed
✔️ British Crown took control from East India Company (1858)

---

## 🔹 2️⃣ Formation of INC (1885)

* Founded by **A.O. Hume**
* First President: **W.C. Bonnerjee**

🎯 Early demand:

* Administrative reforms
* Indian representation

---

## 🔹 3️⃣ Partition of Bengal (1905)

* Done by **Lord Curzon**
* Divided Bengal on communal lines

🔥 Result:

* **Swadeshi Movement**
* Boycott of British goods

---

## 🔹 4️⃣ Home Rule Movement (1916)

* Leaders:

  * **Bal Gangadhar Tilak**
  * **Annie Besant**

🎯 Demand:

* Self-government within British Empire

---

## 🔹 5️⃣ Gandhian Era (1917–1947)

### 🕊️ Entry of Gandhi:

Returned to India in **1915**

---

### 📌 Major Movements (VERY IMPORTANT)

#### 🟢 Champaran Satyagraha (1917)

* Indigo farmers (Bihar)

#### 🟢 Non-Cooperation Movement (1920)

* Boycott schools, courts
* Withdrawn after **Chauri Chaura incident (1922)**

#### 🟢 Civil Disobedience Movement (1930)

* Started with **Dandi March**
* Salt Law broken

#### 🟢 Quit India Movement (1942)

* Slogan: **“Do or Die”**
* Mass uprising

---

## 🔹 6️⃣ Revolutionary Activities

### 🔥 Important revolutionaries:

* **Bhagat Singh**
* **Chandrashekhar Azad**
* **Sukhdev**
* **Rajguru**

📌 Key event:

* Assembly bomb case (1929)

---

## 🔹 7️⃣ INA & Subhas Chandra Bose

* Founded **Indian National Army**
* Slogan: **“Give me blood, I will give you freedom”**

---

## 🔹 8️⃣ Independence & Partition (1947)

* Independence: **15 August 1947**
* Last Viceroy: **Lord Mountbatten**
* First PM: **Jawaharlal Nehru**
