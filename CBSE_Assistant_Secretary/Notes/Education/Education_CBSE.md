## 📘 1. CBSE as an Organization

**CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education)**  
- An **autonomous organization** under the **Ministry of Education**
- Established in **1962**
- Headquarters: **New Delhi**
- Conducts examinations for:
  - **Classes X & XII**
  - Various **eligibility & recruitment exams**
- Affiliated with:
  - Government schools
  - Kendriya Vidyalayas
  - Private schools (India & abroad)

### Key Functions
- Prescribing syllabus & curriculum
- Conducting board examinations
- Academic reforms & assessment design
- Teacher training & capacity building

---

## 🏛️ 2. Ministry of Education (MoE)

- Former name: **Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD)**
- Renamed in **2020**
- Responsible for:
  - School education
  - Higher education
  - Educational policies

### Two Departments
1. **Department of School Education & Literacy**
2. **Department of Higher Education**

📌 CBSE works under the **Department of School Education & Literacy**

---

## 📜 3. National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 – Basics

- Approved in **July 2020**
- Replaced **NEP 1986**
- Focus: **Access, Equity, Quality, Accountability**

### Major School-Level Reforms
- New structure: **5+3+3+4**
- Emphasis on:
  - Conceptual learning
  - Skill-based education
  - Multilingualism
- Coding & vocational exposure from early classes
- Board exams to test **competency**, not rote learning

## 🧱 4. School Education Structure in India

### Old Structure
- 10+2 system

### New Structure (NEP 2020)
| Stage        | Classes        | Age Group |
|--------------|----------------|-----------|
| Foundational | Pre-school–2   | 3–8 yrs   |
| Preparatory  | 3–5            | 8–11 yrs  |
| Middle       | 6–8            | 11–14 yrs |
| Secondary    | 9–12           | 14–18 yrs |

📌 Board exams at **Class 10 & 12** continue

## 🏫 5. Boards & Major School Systems

### 📗 CBSE
- National-level board
- English & Hindi medium
- Uniform syllabus across India

### 🏫 Kendriya Vidyalayas (KVs)
- Under **Ministry of Education**
- Managed by **KVS (Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan)**
- Mainly for **central government employees’ children**
- Follow **CBSE curriculum**

### 🏡 Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNVs)
- Fully **residential schools**
- For **rural & talented students**
- Managed by **Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti**
- Admission through **JNVST**
- Follow **CBSE curriculum**
