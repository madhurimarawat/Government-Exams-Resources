## 🖥️ Types of Computers — WITH CLEAR EXAMPLES

### 🧮 **Microcomputer**

👉 Small, single-user computers used for everyday tasks.

**Examples:**

* 💻 Desktop Computer
* 🧑‍💻 Personal Computer (PC)
* 🍎 Apple iMac
* 💡 Raspberry Pi

---

### 🧑‍💻 **Personal Computer (PC)**

👉 General-purpose computer for one user.

**Examples:**

* 🪟 Windows PC
* 🍎 Apple MacBook
* 🐧 Linux Desktop

---

### 🖥️ **Workstation**

👉 High-performance computer for technical or professional work.

**Examples:**

* 🧠 HP Z Series
* ⚙️ Dell Precision
* 🎨 Used for CAD, 3D modeling, data analysis

---

### 🏢 **Minicomputer**

👉 Medium-sized computer supporting multiple users at the same time
*(Mostly used earlier; now largely replaced by servers)*

**Examples:**

* 🗄️ DEC PDP-11
* 🏛️ IBM AS/400
* 🏭 Used in industries and labs

---

### 🏦 **Mainframe Computer**

👉 Very powerful computers used by large organizations to handle huge data and thousands of users.

**Examples (IMPORTANT):**

* 🏦 IBM Z Series
* 💳 Used by Banks (SBI, ICICI, HDFC)
* ✈️ Airline Reservation Systems
* 🏛️ Government Census Systems

---

### 🚀 **Supercomputer**

👉 Fastest and most powerful computers used for complex calculations.

**Indian Examples 🇮🇳:**

* 🇮🇳 PARAM Siddhi-AI
* 🌦️ Pratyush & Mihir
* 🤖 AIRAWAT

**Global Examples 🌍:**

* 🇺🇸 Frontier (USA)
* 🇯🇵 Fugaku (Japan)
* 🇫🇮 LUMI (Finland)
